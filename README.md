# Scholar's Budget

### 1. Problem
As an international student studying at an overseas university, managing finances can be difficult. From the differences in exchange rates to the difficulties in transferral of money from one country to another. Keeping track of expenses, tuition, housing, food, and more is certainly tenfold more difficult for international students than local students with the added difficulties of the exchange rate differences and the lack of support. 

Here are some examples of problems international students might face in dealing with finances:
* Currency fluctuation: In 2023–2024, the Central Bank of Nigeria moved to unify its exchange rate, causing the Naira to plummet by nearly 60% against the US Dollar and British Pound almost overnight. Students like "Chidi," who had budgeted ₦10 million for tuition (roughly £15,000 at the time), suddenly found they needed ₦18 million to cover the same bill just months later.
